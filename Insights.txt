Business Insights of the Northwind Dataset

1. Top 3 Products Drive Nearly Half of Revenue
* Insight: The top 3 highest-revenue products account for approximately 45% of total sales, highlighting that a small number of SKUs are critical for overall revenue.
2. Significant Portion of Customers Are Inactive
* Insight: Around 30% of customers have never placed an order, representing a large pool of potential buyers who could be targeted through promotions or re-engagement campaigns.
3. Revenue is Concentrated in Key Categories
* Insight: The top 2 product categories generate about 60% of total revenue, showing that category-level performance is highly skewed and focusing on these categories could maximize profitability.
4. Certain Regions Dominate Sales
* Insight: Sales in Germany and Washington together account for roughly 40% of total revenue, indicating that marketing and shipping strategies should prioritize these high-performing regions.
